About-us

Believes in quality

The Farouk educational trust is a registered organization,
established in the year 2003 by the founder and chairman Er. Mohammed B. Farouk , also chairman of Farouk Bros & Co, in Bangkok and Hong Kong.
His vision is to provide quality education with international standards to various categories of students and make them more potential and self-reliant.
This has paved way to the formation of Farouk Educational Trust which successful runs

  • RAAK Arts and Science College, affliated to Thiruvalluvar University, Vellore.
  • RAAK College of Engineering and Technology, affliated to Pondicherry University.
  • RAAk Nursing and Paramedical College, affliated to Pondicherry University.
  • RAAK Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology, Collaborative progamme with Alagappa University, Karaikudi.
  • RAAK International Higher Secondary School, approved by Govt. of Puducherry.

    • We offer a very lively learning experience to around 3000 scholars every year.
    Our Trust has laid its landmark for itself in the educational hub of both Tamil Nadu and union territory of Pondicherry
